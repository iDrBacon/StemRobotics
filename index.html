Welcome to STEM Robotics!

Welcome to the STEM Robotics site! STEM Robotics is the engineering part of STEM: Science,Technology,Engineering,and Math! We use Lego Mindstorms NXT to make our robots. We make challenges, design robots, and then test/program those robots to defeat the challenge we previously had made up.

Stem Robotics is a class at the STEM Acadamy at Nimitz Middle school.

Our current challenge is to make a robot follow a line, while pushing a ball to a certain line. The robot must NOT go over the line, but the ball must.

What we do

We design robots, thats ovbious. Our robots use Lego Mindstorms NXT 2.0. We also use metal. We also do competitions, like SABest, First Lego League, and GEAR.
